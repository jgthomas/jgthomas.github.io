My pivot

Hi, I'm James!

This site is the record of my pivot from academic to software engineer.

Two things happened in early 2017 that I didn't think were connected, at least not at the time. The first was me realising the academic world was not for me. The second was a growing desire for a new hobby—a creative outlet of some kind.

My thoughts drifted back to the programming I did during the first years of my PhD. I had forgotten pretty much everything, but I did remember really enjoying it. I started playing with code again. What began as a hobby soon became a runaway obsession, which I have since ridden through another degree, volunteer work, and into my new professional life.

I could definitely have been here earlier. As a child I played with Visual Basic, getting up early to shift elements around. Somehow, though, it didn't 'stick'. It has been my fortune, therefore, to pick up a childlike obsession with programming and computer science as an adult, with all the advantages and disadvantages that come with that timing.

So, you could say I'm something of a “late developer”...but I'm here now!
