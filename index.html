Me

Reader, I pivoted.

After spending much time somewhere between the arts and the sciences, I decided to learn a craft. This site represents the fruits of my apprenticeship thus far.

I first encountered programming during my journey round the foothills of academia. Yes, I'm one of those people. Actually, this is not entirely true. I played with Visual Basic as a child, getting up early to shift elements around. Somehow, though, it didn't 'stick'.

It has been my fortune, therefore, to pick up a child-like obession with programming and computer science as an adult, with all the advantages and disadvantages that come with that timing.
